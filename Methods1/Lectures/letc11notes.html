1.3.2 Plot a scatterplot log(Time - 3100) vs. Year

prob2b.pdf

  • Write out a linear model to study the relationship between log(Time - 3100) and Year.
    Interpret your two parameters in the model. \begin{align*} \mathrm{Let} Y_i &= log(\mathrm{Time}_i -3100)\\ \mathrm{Model:} Y_i = \beta_0 + \beta_1 X_i + \epsilon_i\\ \hat{\beta}_1 &= \frac{S_{xy}}{S_{xx}} \\ &=\frac{ \sum (X_i - \bar{X})(Y_i - \bar{Y})}{\sum (X_i - \bar{X})^2}\\ &= -0.03\\ \hat{\beta}_{0} &= \bar(Y) - \hat{\beta}_{1}\bar{X}\\ &= 65.64 \end{align*}

    Interpretation: β0 tells us the value of Y when/if X equals 0. So in year 0, we would expect \(\log(\mathrm{Time} - 3100)\) to be approximately 66. Solving for Time gives 3.2226970275426e+28.

    β1 tells us the magnitude of increase in \(\log(\mathrm{Time} - 3100)\) for a 1 year increase in time. Since β0 is negative, it is actually a decrease. Solving for Time gives 3100.97
